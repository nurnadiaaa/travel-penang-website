Batu Ferringhi

Batu Ferringhi is a stunning coastal destination known for its sandy beaches, turquoise waters, and vibrant nightlife. It offers water sports, a lively night market, and a mix of luxury resorts and local eateries, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and adventure.

Entopia

Entopia is a unique nature attraction featuring a vibrant butterfly and insect sanctuary. It offers interactive exhibits, lush gardens, and educational experiences, making it a must-visit for nature lovers and families.

Escape Penang

Escape Penang is an exciting adventure park offering thrilling outdoor activities for all ages. It features high ropes courses, water slides, zip lines, and Malaysia's longest water slide. Perfect for adrenaline seekers and families, Escape combines fun, nature, and adventure in a lush, green setting.
